About Accountability

Project Description

We believe that accountability, fairness and transparency are crucial when it comes to managing chores and creating healthy living spaces with your roommates.

We decided to create Accountability when we noticed the need for a way to easily and fairly distribute chore responsibilities, as well as hold roommates accountable to them.

From our interviews, respondents most wanted a way to track each roommate’s chore completions, in order to understand if each roommate was doing their fair share. Additionally, they called for an easy and non-personal way to decide chore duties and hold one another responsible for them.

Features

In Accountability, you can easily manage chore duties between roommates. A chore can be created by any member of the group. Besides a name, a user may include a reward point, and either choose an assignee manually or randomly for a chore, based on the total chore points earned. When chores are created, they are shown with reward points to all members of the group. Users earn chore points by completing the duty and tapping on the corresponding chore on the application. Also, users can view which tasks are done by whom. They may send ‘like’ to send a compliment. In addition, the app displays the member with the most reward points on a monthly basis. By earning the points, a user may earn a reward such as a free chore pass.

By using Accountability, we wish these features can encourage users to complete their duties while they feel enjoyable completing each chore.
